Add a new enterprise-grade Reporting, Forecasting, and AI Insights module to the existing FinanceOS platform.

Goal:
Replace manual SQL-to-Excel reporting and forecasting workflows with a governed, visual, AI-driven reporting and planning layer that produces executive dashboards, board-ready reports, rolling forecasts, scenario models, variance narratives, anomaly detection, and AI-generated business insights with recommendations for cost reduction and margin improvement.

This module must sit on top of the existing architecture:
- semantic layer for structured metrics
- RAG/document layer for supporting evidence and commentary grounding
- workflow/agent layer for automation and recommendations
- governance layer for approvals, auditability, and access control

Do NOT connect to a live database, vector DB, or LLM yet.
Build schemas, interfaces, mock adapters, UI shells, and evaluation harnesses so I can plug in the real infrastructure later.

- make reports and forecasts explainable, drillable, and presentation-ready
